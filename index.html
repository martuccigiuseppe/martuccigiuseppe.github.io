About me

I am a Ph.D. student in Information Technology (Computer Science area) at Politecnico di Milano, expecting to graduate in 2023.

I hold a B.Sc. and an M.Sc. in Automation and Control Engineering by Politecnico di Milano.

I am currently a member of the DEEP-SE group at the Department of Electronics, Information, and Bioengineering (DEIB). My main research interests are formal modeling and verification techniques of human-robot interaction in service settings.
